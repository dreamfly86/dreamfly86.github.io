Shanhao Zhan received the B.S. degree in Communication Engineering from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China in 2022. He is currently pursuing a M.S. degree in Communication Engineering at Xiamen University in China. His research interests include the Internet of Vehicles, ISAC, resource management in 6G networks, graph representation learning, graph reinforcement learning, and multi-agent reinforcement learning.
